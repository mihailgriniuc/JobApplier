📋 User Guide

Getting Started

Initial Setup

  1. Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Read and accept the privacy consent
  3. Complete the 4-step setup wizard:
    • Step 1: Enter your personal information
    • Step 2: Answer work authorization questions
    • Step 3: Provide demographic information (optional)
    • Step 4: Upload your resume
  4. Click "Save & Finish" to complete setup
Tip: You can update your information anytime by clicking "Edit Info" in the popup or visiting the Options page.

Using Autofill

Method 1: Toolbar Button

  1. Navigate to a job application page
  2. Click the extension icon in your toolbar
  3. Click "Fill Application Form"

Method 2: On-Page Button

When you visit a job application page, a floating "Autofill" button appears in the bottom-right corner. Click it to fill the form.

Method 3: Keyboard Shortcut

Platform Shortcut
Mac ⌘ + Shift + F
Windows/Linux Ctrl + Shift + F

Optional AI Answers

  1. Open Options and go to Settings
  2. Turn on "Enable AI answers"
  3. Paste your Mistral API key and choose a model
  4. Optionally paste extra resume highlights for richer answers
  5. Run autofill on a form that contains an open-ended text question
Note: AI answers are opt-in. When enabled, the extension sends the question, job-posting excerpt, saved profile fields, and your optional extra context to Mistral to draft an answer.

Supported Job Sites

The extension automatically activates on major job platforms:

  • LinkedIn Jobs
  • Indeed
  • Glassdoor
  • Lever (jobs.lever.co)
  • Greenhouse (boards.greenhouse.io)
  • Workday
  • ZipRecruiter
  • Monster
  • And many more...

It also works on company career pages that contain "careers", "jobs", or "apply" in the URL.

Managing Your Data

View & Edit Data

  1. Click "View Data" in the popup, or
  2. Right-click the extension icon → Options

Export Data

  1. Go to Options → Import/Export tab
  2. Click "Export to JSON"
  3. Save the file to backup your data

Import Data

  1. Go to Options → Import/Export tab
  2. Drag and drop a previously exported JSON file

Delete All Data

  1. Go to Options → Profile Data tab
  2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
  3. Click "Delete All Data"
Warning: Deleting your data cannot be undone. Export a backup first if you might need your data later.

Troubleshooting

Fields not being detected

  • Some forms use custom or unusual field naming that may not be recognized
  • Try clicking on the field first to focus it, then use autofill
  • Multi-page forms may need autofill triggered on each page

Resume not uploading

  • Ensure your resume is PDF, DOC, or DOCX format
  • File must be under 5MB
  • Some sites may not support automated file uploads

Autofill button not appearing

  • The page may not be recognized as a job application
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Use the toolbar icon or keyboard shortcut instead

Privacy & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent externally unless you turn on AI answers
  • You control when and where your data is used
  • Delete your data anytime in the Options page
